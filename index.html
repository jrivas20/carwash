About Latin Car Wash


If you’re in need of great car wash service or car detailing, Latin Car Wash is the place to go, we go anywhere. Our flexible convenient system to go where your car is make it easy to get exactly what you need to ensure that your car stays in amazing shape. We take a lot of pride in always being able to provide what our customers need, when they need it. When we come visit to you, you’ll be able to see why we’re the only ones that will get your car washed or detailed.
Latin Car Wash is a Flex Full Service Car Wash One of the traits that sets Latin Car Wash apart is our unique car wash environment. The most common approach that car washes take is to either offer a completely automated experience or an exclusively hands-on service. The problem with making customers choose between these two types of car washes is there’s no way to get the best results. When a car wash is fully automated, the lower price point is likely to be attractive. But the results of a car wash that doesn’t involve any human touch almost always end up disappointing. While that’s not an issue with an exclusively hands-on service, only utilizing human labor instead of any technology means these car washes have to charge steep rates for their services.
